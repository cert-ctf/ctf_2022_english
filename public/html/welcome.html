Welcome

We are pleased to welcome you to our event map for the Capture The Flag event.

To help you get started in the virtual world, we have provided basic information here.

Control

As in a computer game, you can move freely around the map with the keyboard.



You can also click on a point on the map with the right mouse button to steer your character there.



Room plan and agenda

In the reception area you will find the room plan and the agenda for today's event. Please do not miss the welcome at the stage, which is scheduled for 09:45h....



Communication

In certain areas of the map, a communication platform is displayed in the browser, which is provided for presentations, assistance and exchange with gematik and our partners.



Communication on the Team Map

We have provided team rooms for you, which you can reach behind your assigned door in the upper area of the map. There you will find an exciting storyline for the event, in which you have to solve "The Secret of the ePA-TI".

As soon as you step near a team member, a communication instance will be created between you and displayed as an iFrame in the browser.



Access to Ti-Park and your team rooms

The gate to the TI-Park, on which your assigned team door is located, will be opened at 10:00 am and released with the welcome at the stage. Please use the doors to go to your assigned team room to play the storyline.



CTFd platform

The event will take place on two levels, once you solve the storyline, more tasks will be unlocked on the CTFd platform. The first task in CTFd "Find all the lost parts from last night" will be unlocked in time for the opening of the TI Park.



any questions?

Our team will be at your disposal during the event, we are looking forward to your visit at the helpdesk.
