Map Overview

The map is divided into several areas:



Entrance area

Your adventure begins in the entrance area of the event level.



TI Ranger and Partner Area

In this area you will find the helpdesk, the gematik TI_ctf as well as the partner area..



Stage

In the stage area the event is presented and released.



Access to TI Park

In this area you can enter the TI Park and find the tasks to the TI_eRX. Bite do not enter this area until cleared by the TI rangers.

Your assigned door to the TI park was provided to you with your workadventure invitation.



Helpdesk

In this area, TI rangers are ready to assist you.



Kitchen and outdoor

These zones have a global chat function. All participants can communicate with each other here.



Icons

On the map, zones where your camera is activated are outlined with a green marker and indicated with a speaker icon.
